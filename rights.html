Rights

Keystone Literary Agency actively manages a range of rights on behalf of our authors, ensuring their work reaches audiences around the world and across multiple platforms. Our rights department focuses on building strong, long-term relationships with trusted partners in publishing, translation, and media.

We represent subsidiary, translation, audio, and film/TV rights for select titles and authors. Our goal is to extend the reach of each project through thoughtful placement and collaboration with international publishers, production companies, and creative teams.

For rights inquiries or to request information about available titles, please contact our rights department at:

rights@keystoneliteraryagency.com

We welcome inquiries from foreign publishers, scouts, producers, and agencies seeking to acquire or represent rights to our authors’ works.
